BUILDING ASSIMP (on a mac) :
git clone https://github.com/assimp/assimp
cd assimp
#you can use arm64 to build for arm
cmake -DASSIMP_NO_EXPORT=1 -DASSIMP_BUILD_TESTS=0 -DCMAKE_OSX_ARCHITECTURES=x86_64 .
cmake --build .
